<h2 id="3a-logo-pizza-delivered">3a - Logo Pizza delivered</h2>

<p><a href="http://logo.pizza"><img src="https://i.imgur.com/u40AMfs.png" alt="Logo Pizza"></a></p>
<p>After shipping <a href="/blog/flickity-v2-released">Flickity v2</a> thus wrapping 2016&apos;s huge development project, I didn&apos;t have it in me to write another line of code. I discussed my state of mind and motivation in this 3 min podcast.</p>
<p><a class="embedly-card" href="https://bumpers.fm/e/avbd99o4m4ug00o93t30">New thing, shortsightedness on Bumpers</a></p>
<script async src="//cdn.embedly.com/widgets/platform.js"></script>

<p>Looking to change things up, I started making logos. </p>
<p>Man, I love logos. A little piece of imagery that represents the ideal you want your project to be &#x2014; that&apos;s design magic right there. I&apos;ve been able to work on some great logo projects, but I&apos;ve been itching to do more. Rather than wait for projects to come my way, I gave myself a project of my own: design 50 logos. <strong>50 logos in 30 days.</strong></p>
<p>Fifty logos is a lot. At least one or two a day for an entire month. I tried pushing myself: exploring different styles, subjects, and techniques. It was like design boot camp: working all those muscles you never use.</p>
