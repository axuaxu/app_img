<h2 class="article__title" id="1a-infinite-scroll-full-page-demo">1a - Infinite Scroll full page demo</h2>

<p><b>Hi!</b> This demo will show off several key features of Infinite Scroll.</p>

<ul>
<li>Full page scrolling</li>
<li>Changing browser URL and history. Watch how the URL changes as you scroll. Try refreshing on a changed page.</li>
<li>Embeds like CodePens and Tweets have their <code>&lt;script&gt;</code>s loaded and embeds rendered</li>
</ul>
